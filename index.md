# Welcome to Neureonmindflux Research Lab

Neureonmindflux is a research laboratory focused on advanced artificial intelligence, post-quantum security, hybrid encryption systems, and conscious agent architectures.

We aim to develop and share high-impact open research tools, frameworks, and publications that empower scientific innovation across fields such as:

- Quantum cryptography
- Secure AI systems
- Cognitive modeling
- Autonomous decision architectures
